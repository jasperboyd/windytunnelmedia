Biography

The Purest Cacophony is the solo music venture by Jasper Boyd. Influenced by contemporary musicians like Sam Amidon, Brown Bird, and The Avett Brothers Jasper wanted to make the transition from accompanist as a bassist to a more central role in the music-making process, and thus Purest Cacophony was born.

With the help of some talented friends, namelely Jonathan Dana, Theo Van Dyke Mike Tierney and Paul Puiia, The Purest Cacophony released its debut EP I Will Not Fall available on bandcamp. More releases lay on the horizon.
