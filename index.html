about wtm

We are a DIY media publisher based in Worcester, MA with artists from all over New England. We are dedicated to bringing their music to you.

The inspiration for the creation of Windy Tunnel Media came from a gift I was given by my grandmother, David Byrne's book "How Music Works". In the book, Byrne speaks at great length about how the music industry is transforming. The recording giants of last century are wilting away. Thanks to technology musicians don't need the huge support systems that record companies used to supply. Artists can do all of that work for themselves and DIY labels like WTM and countless others are replacing those buildings.

Most simply Windy Tunnel Media is a symbiotic collective of aspiring musicians. The reason for this is that I've never viewed music as a competition and I wanted WTM to embody that belief. Therefore, WTM works not only within itself but with other artists from other DIY labels, in all aspects of the process. By listening and supporting just a single WTM artist you are in effect supporting the entire organization.

Although WTM is a profitless publisher in the more traditional sense of the word "profit", by sharing our label you are supporting the most fundamental part of the label, the artists. So please, have a listen on our music page and give some feedback. If you want to do even more consider donating .

Thank you,
Jasper

3 Ways to Help

Pay the Musicians

Being a WTM Musician isn't exactly profitable. For the most part all the releases are pay-what-you-wish and the grand majority of people opt for the free download option. If you find yourself really liking any one particular artist's music here please consider giving some money even if it's only a couple dollars.

Donate to Windy Tunnel

WTM although extremely lightweight costs money too. Server space and enormous amount of free work went into the creation of this website. To help keep us running any small contribution can help.

If Nothing Else

Help spread the word! Like and share our page on facebook. Read and link to our blog. Most importantly give us feedback!
