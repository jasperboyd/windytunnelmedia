Biography

Missing Twin is Samantha Martasian. Missing Twin is inspired by Paul Baribeau, The Mountain Goats, and Stephen Malkmus.

Missing Twin is a Project about living in a house with a lot of spiders.

Missing Twin is based in Boston and often shares the stage with FORT! The Band, James Clifford, Andrew Lowery and Kismet.
